Frequently Asked Questions

Your Go-To for Quick and Clear Answers.

What services does SiteSphere offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services including custom website development, responsive web design, web application development, SEO, and professional copywriting.

How much does a new website cost?

The cost of a website varies depending on the complexity, features, and design requirements. Contact us for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for building a website depends on the project's scope and complexity. On average, a standard website can take anywhere from 4 to 12 weeks from the initial consultation to launch.

Can you work with my existing website?

Absolutely! We can help you redesign, update, or optimize your existing website to better meet your needs and improve its performance.

What kind of support do you offer after the website is launched?

We offer ongoing support and maintenance services to keep your website running smoothly. This includes updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and technical support.
